EVOLUTION OF THANKUFOODS
What started as a single street-side kiosk and the simple idea of making and selling cookies made by blind youth, has now grown into a pan-India brand delivering high quality, healthy, and tasty snacks. Today, our products have delighted more than 100000+ customers. Our differently-abled youth manage kiosks at 500+ top corporates across the country.

KNOW TOUR THANKU'ER
Punithavathi, born in Thiruvannamalai district, is a visually challenged person by birth due to consanguineous marriage. Earning her bachelor's degree in History, she aspires to become a radio jockey. She is the sole breadwinner in her family. Remember, She is partially blind so if you want to convey something, please make sure that she uses her ears.
Marichelvam, born in Sattur, was partially blind from birth. He lost his eye sight totally after his eye surgery went wrong. But still he has the determination to face all the challenges that life throws at him. He has an English Bachelor's degree. He is one of the breadwinners in his family. Remember, he is totally blind so if you want to convey something, Please make sure that he uses his ears.
